Experimental Setup

This section reports experimental details and analyses omitted from the main manuscript due to page constraints. All experiments are designed to assess the contribution of geometry-informed components—namely spectral graph embeddings and spatial regularization— to GNSS-R data interpolation performance.

  • Dataset: CYGNSS GNSS-R observations
  • Training / validation split consistent with main paper

Ablation Studies

We conduct controlled ablation experiments to isolate the effect of each architectural component. In particular, we analyze the removal of:

  • Graph-based spectral positional encodings
  • Geometry-aware regularization terms
  • Edge-aware sharpening mechanisms
